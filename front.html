OUR MISSION:

At Lunar Nova, we are passionate about exploring the frontiers of space science and technology to better understand planetary seismic activities. Our mission is to develop innovative machine learning solutions to predict seismic events, using data from some of humanity's greatest explorations—the Apollo missions and NASA’s Mars InSight Lander.

MACHINE LEARNING: THE NEW WAVE IN SEISMIC DETECTION

Our platform combines state-of-the-art algorithms with real-world data from these missions to detect and predict seismic events. We analyze continuous seismic records, leveraging both traditional techniques like Short-term Average to Long-term Average (STA/LTA) and advanced machine learning models. Our goal is to create accurate forecasts of seismic activity by identifying patterns and anomalies that could signify seismic events, even uncovering events not previously cataloged.
